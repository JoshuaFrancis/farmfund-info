Platform overview

Three pillars that move capital from investor to field.

01

Farm loan origination

A direct-to-farmer lending portal where operators apply for financing against land equity, crop yield history, and operating cash flow. Automated underwriting scores farm credit risk using satellite imagery, soil data, and commodity price feeds to produce consistent loan decisions at scale with minimal manual review overhead.

02

Investor syndication

A co-investment layer allowing accredited investors to participate in individual farm loans or diversified loan pools, with real-time portfolio dashboards showing loan status, repayment schedules, and projected yield. Structured to meet Canadian exempt-market dealer requirements and built for integration with registered account providers.

03

Agri-data intelligence

A proprietary agricultural scoring layer that aggregates AAFC crop data, provincial land registry records, weather history, and commodity futures to produce a farm-level risk and return index. Used internally for underwriting and available as a licensed data product to lenders, insurers, and institutional allocators building agri-asset exposure.
